Barbie The Movie: Characters

Barbie

Margot Robbie

The stereotypical Barbie, she’s everything you’d expect—beautiful, confident, and always dressed to impress. But when she starts questioning her perfect life in Barbieland, she embarks on an eye-opening journey to the real world. Through laughter, adventure, and self-discovery, she realizes that being Barbie is more than just looking perfect—it’s about embracing who you truly are.

Ken

Ryan Gosling

Ken has always lived in Barbie’s shadow, but he’s ready for his own moment in the spotlight! Lovable, clueless, and always enthusiastic, Ken follows Barbie into the real world, where he begins to explore his own identity and purpose. With his endless charm and over-the-top fashion sense, Ken proves he’s more than just an accessory!
