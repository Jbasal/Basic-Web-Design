Barbie

Barbie is about a doll that lives in perfect Barbie land. She has a perfect life every day with her fellow Barbies and her boyfriend Ken. Her life suddenly turns upside down when there is trouble in the real world. Barbie must travel to the real world and face her darkest fears.

Random facts

Billie Eilish wrote the song called “What Was I Made For?”. This song from Barbie the album won the Academy award for best music (original song), Grammy award for a song of the year, a golden globe award for best original song and more. The Barbie movie was the highest grossing movie of 2023. Barbie was the first live action movie based on a Barbie doll.
